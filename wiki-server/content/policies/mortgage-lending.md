# Mortgage Lending Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-ML-002
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Revised:** March 15, 2026

## Overview

This policy governs all residential mortgage lending at Solo Bank, including conventional conforming loans, jumbo loans, and refinancing. All mortgage decisions must comply with federal regulations (TILA, RESPA, ECOA) and this internal policy.

## Eligibility Requirements

### Minimum Credit Score

- **Minimum FICO score for mortgage eligibility:** 580 (Tier 4)
- Tier 5 customers (FICO below 580) are **not eligible** for any mortgage products.
- See [Credit Score Tiers](credit-score-tiers.md) for full tier definitions.

### Debt-to-Income Ratio (DTI)

- **Maximum DTI:** 43% for all mortgage products
- DTI is calculated as total monthly debt obligations (including the proposed mortgage payment) divided by gross monthly income.
- For borrowers with DTI between 40% and 43%, compensating factors are required (e.g., 6+ months reserves, strong credit history, significant down payment).

### Employment and Income Verification

- Minimum 2 years of continuous employment or self-employment history.
- Income verified through most recent 2 years of W-2s or tax returns.
- Self-employed borrowers must provide 2 years of business tax returns plus a current profit-and-loss statement.

## Down Payment Requirements

| Credit Tier | Conventional Conforming | Jumbo |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 (800–850) | 5% minimum | 10% minimum |
| Tier 2 (740–799) | 5% minimum | 15% minimum |
| Tier 3 (670–739) | 10% minimum | Not eligible |
| Tier 4 (580–669) | 10% minimum | Not eligible |
| Tier 5 (300–579) | Not eligible | Not eligible |

- Private Mortgage Insurance (PMI) is required when the down payment is less than 20%.
- PMI is automatically removed when the loan-to-value ratio reaches 78%.

## Loan Limits

- **Conforming loan limit:** $766,550 (2026 FHFA limit)
- **Jumbo loan range:** $766,551 to $2,500,000
- Solo Bank does not originate mortgage loans exceeding $2,500,000.
- Jumbo loans are available only to Tier 1 and Tier 2 borrowers.

## Loan Types and Rates

Current rates are published in the [Mortgage Rates Schedule](../rates/mortgage-rates.md). Available loan products:

- **30-Year Fixed Rate** — Available to Tiers 1–4
- **15-Year Fixed Rate** — Available to Tiers 1–4
- **5/1 Adjustable Rate Mortgage (ARM)** — Available to Tiers 1–3 only
- **Jumbo 30-Year Fixed** — Available to Tiers 1–2 only

Rate adjustments by credit tier and relationship discounts are applied per [Interest Rate Schedule](interest-rate-schedule.md).

## Required Documentation

Applicants must provide the following at the time of application:

1. Government-issued photo identification
2. Social Security Number (for credit pull authorization)
3. Most recent 2 years of W-2 forms or tax returns
4. Most recent 2 months of pay stubs
5. Most recent 3 months of bank statements (all accounts)
6. Signed authorization for employment verification
7. Purchase agreement (for purchase loans) or current mortgage statement (for refinances)
8. Proof of homeowners insurance
9. Gift letter (if any portion of down payment is gifted)

## Approval Process

1. **Pre-qualification:** Loan officer reviews self-reported income, debts, and estimated credit score to provide a preliminary assessment. No credit pull at this stage.
2. **Application:** Borrower completes the Uniform Residential Loan Application (Form 1003). Loan officer pulls credit from all three bureaus and assigns credit tier.
3. **Processing:** Processor orders appraisal, title search, and verifies all documentation. Target: 5 business days.
4. **Underwriting:** Underwriter evaluates the complete file against policy requirements. Target: 3 business days.
5. **Conditional Approval:** Underwriter issues conditional approval with a list of conditions to be satisfied (e.g., updated pay stub, explanation letters).
6. **Clear to Close:** All conditions satisfied. Closing disclosure issued. Borrower has a 3-day review period.
7. **Closing:** Documents signed, funds disbursed, mortgage recorded.

- **Tier 1–2 loans within auto-approve limits** may use automated underwriting with manual review override.
- **Tier 3 loans** require full manual underwriting.
- **Tier 4 loans** require full manual underwriting plus Branch Manager sign-off.

## Refinancing Rules

- Borrowers must have made at least 12 on-time payments on their current mortgage before refinancing.
- Cash-out refinances are limited to 80% loan-to-value.
- Rate-and-term refinances follow the same down payment / equity requirements as new purchases.
- No seasoning requirement for rate-and-term refinances from external lenders.
- Appraisal required for all refinances; no appraisal waivers.

## Property Requirements

- Property must be owner-occupied primary residence or second home (no investment properties).
- Appraisal must be completed by a Solo Bank-approved appraiser.
- Minimum property condition standards per FHA/FHFA guidelines must be met.
- Condominiums must be in FHA-approved or warrantable projects.

## Escrow Requirements

- Escrow accounts for property taxes and insurance are required for all loans with LTV above 80%.
- Borrowers with LTV at or below 80% may waive escrow with a 0.25% rate adjustment.
