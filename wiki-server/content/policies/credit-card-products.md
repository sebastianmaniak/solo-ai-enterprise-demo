# Credit Card Products Policy

**Policy ID:** POL-CC-004
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Revised:** March 15, 2026

## Overview

Solo Bank offers three credit card products designed to serve customers across all credit tiers. This policy defines the product matrix, eligibility requirements, fee structures, APR schedules, and credit limit formulas.

## Product Matrix

### Platinum Rewards Card

- **Eligible Tiers:** Tier 1 and Tier 2 only
- **Annual Fee:** $195
- **Rewards:**
  - 3x points per $1 on dining and travel
  - 2x points per $1 on groceries
  - 1x points per $1 on all other purchases
- **APR:**
  - Tier 1: 18.99% variable
  - Tier 2: 20.99% variable
- **Penalty APR:** 29.99% (triggered after 60 days past due)
- **Introductory Offer:** 80,000 bonus points after spending $4,000 in the first 3 months
- **Additional Benefits:** Airport lounge access (Solo Lounges network), travel accident insurance up to $500,000, no foreign transaction fees, primary rental car insurance, purchase protection for 120 days
- **Minimum Credit Limit:** $10,000

### Cashback Card

- **Eligible Tiers:** Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3
- **Annual Fee:** $0
- **Rewards:**
  - 2% unlimited cashback on all purchases
- **APR:**
  - Tier 1: 19.99% variable
  - Tier 2: 21.99% variable
  - Tier 3: 24.99% variable
- **Penalty APR:** 29.99% (triggered after 60 days past due)
- **Introductory Offer:** $200 statement credit after spending $1,000 in the first 3 months
- **Additional Benefits:** No foreign transaction fees, free FICO score monitoring, cell phone protection with card payment of monthly bill
- **Minimum Credit Limit:** $2,000

### Secured Card

- **Eligible Tiers:** All tiers (Tier 1 through Tier 5)
- **Annual Fee:** $39
- **Rewards:**
  - 1% cashback on all purchases
- **APR:** 22.99% fixed (same for all tiers)
- **Penalty APR:** 29.99% (triggered after 60 days past due)
- **Security Deposit:** Minimum $200, maximum $5,000. Credit limit equals the deposit amount.
- **Additional Benefits:** Monthly reporting to all three credit bureaus, free FICO score monitoring
- **Upgrade Path:** After 12 consecutive months of on-time payments and a credit score improvement to Tier 3 or above, the cardholder is eligible for an automatic upgrade to the Cashback Card with deposit refund.

## Credit Limit Formulas

Credit limits are determined by the customer's tier and verified gross annual salary:

| Tier | Maximum Credit Limit Formula | Hard Cap |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 | 50% of gross annual salary | None |
| Tier 2 | 40% of gross annual salary | None |
| Tier 3 | 30% of gross annual salary | None |
| Tier 4 | 20% of gross annual salary | $10,000 |
| Tier 5 | Equal to security deposit | $5,000 |

### Credit Limit Examples

- **Tier 1 customer earning $120,000/year:** Maximum credit limit = $60,000
- **Tier 2 customer earning $85,000/year:** Maximum credit limit = $34,000
- **Tier 3 customer earning $65,000/year:** Maximum credit limit = $19,500
- **Tier 4 customer earning $55,000/year:** Calculated limit = $11,000, but hard cap applies, so maximum = $10,000
- **Tier 5 customer with $1,000 deposit:** Credit limit = $1,000

## Credit Limit Increases

- Automatic credit limit reviews occur every 6 months.
- Customers may request a credit limit increase at any time; the request triggers a soft credit pull.
- Increases are subject to the same tier-based formulas and caps.
- No credit limit increase will be granted if the account has had a late payment in the prior 6 months.

## Cash Advance Terms

- Cash advance fee: 5% of the amount (minimum $10)
- Cash advance APR: Purchase APR + 4.00% for all cards
- No grace period on cash advances — interest accrues from the transaction date.
- Cash advance limit: 30% of the total credit limit.

## Balance Transfer Terms

- Balance transfer fee: 3% of the amount (minimum $5)
- Balance transfers are available on the Platinum Rewards and Cashback cards only.
- Promotional balance transfer APR of 0% for 15 months is available for Tier 1 and Tier 2 customers on new card openings.

## Late Payment Fees

- 1st late payment in 12 months: $29
- Subsequent late payments within 12 months: $40
- Late payment fee will not exceed the minimum payment amount.
