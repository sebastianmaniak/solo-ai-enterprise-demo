# Interest Rate Schedule

**Policy ID:** POL-IR-003
**Effective Date:** April 1, 2026
**Last Revised:** April 1, 2026

## Overview

Solo Bank determines lending interest rates using a formulaic approach that combines a base rate with credit tier adjustments, product-specific adjustments, and relationship discounts. This policy defines the formula and provides worked examples for each credit tier.

## Rate Formula

```
Final Rate = Base Rate + Credit Tier Adjustment + Product Adjustment - Relationship Discount
```

### Components

1. **Base Rate:** Set monthly by the Solo Bank Asset-Liability Committee (ALCO) based on the Federal Funds Rate and market conditions. The current base rate is published internally and updated on the first business day of each month.

2. **Credit Tier Adjustment:** Determined by the borrower's FICO-based tier assignment per [Credit Score Tiers](credit-score-tiers.md):

   | Tier | Score Range | Adjustment |
   |---|---|---|
   | Tier 1 — Excellent | 800–850 | +0.000% |
   | Tier 2 — Very Good | 740–799 | +0.250% |
   | Tier 3 — Good | 670–739 | +0.750% |
   | Tier 4 — Fair | 580–669 | +1.500% |
   | Tier 5 — Poor | 300–579 | +3.000% |

3. **Product Adjustment:** Each lending product carries its own spread above the base. See specific product rate sheets for the current product adjustment amounts.

4. **Relationship Discount:** Applied based on the customer's account tenure:

   | Tenure | Discount |
   |---|---|
   | Less than 5 years | 0.000% |
   | 5–9 years | -0.125% |
   | 10+ years | -0.250% |

## Current Base Rate

As of April 1, 2026, the Solo Bank base rate for mortgage products is **6.125%** (30-year fixed reference). Other product base rates are derived from this reference rate.

## Rate Lock Policy

- Rates may be locked for 30, 45, or 60 days from the date of application.
- Lock extensions beyond 60 days incur a 0.125% fee per 15-day extension.
- Rate locks are non-transferable between loan products.
- If rates drop after lock, borrowers may request a one-time float-down at a cost of 0.250% of the loan amount.

## Rate Change Authority

- Base rate changes require ALCO committee approval (meets monthly, or as needed).
- Product adjustment changes require Chief Lending Officer approval.
- Relationship discount tiers require Board of Directors approval for any modification.
