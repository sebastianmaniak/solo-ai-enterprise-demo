# Account Types

**Policy ID:** POL-AT-008
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Revised:** March 1, 2026

## Overview

Solo Bank offers a range of deposit account products to meet the needs of customers at every financial stage. This document describes each account type, its features, eligibility requirements, and key terms. For current interest rates, see the [Savings Rates](../rates/savings-rates.md) and [CD Rates](../rates/cd-rates.md) schedules.

## Checking Accounts

### Basic Checking

- **Monthly Maintenance Fee:** $12.00, waived with a $1,500 minimum daily balance or qualifying direct deposit of $500 or more per month
- **Minimum Opening Deposit:** $25
- **Interest Bearing:** No
- **Debit Card:** Visa debit card included
- **Mobile Banking:** Full mobile banking and mobile check deposit included
- **Online Bill Pay:** Included at no additional charge
- **Check Writing:** Standard check order $25 per box
- **ATM Access:** Free at all Solo Bank ATMs; $3.00 per out-of-network domestic ATM transaction
- **Overdraft Options:** Eligible for Standard Overdraft, Overdraft Protection (linked savings), and Overdraft Line of Credit
- **Paper Statements:** Free (mailed monthly); electronic statements also available
- **Eligibility:** Available to all customers who pass KYC/AML verification and ChexSystems review. No minimum credit score required.

### Premium Checking

- **Monthly Maintenance Fee:** $0 with $5,000 minimum daily balance; $25.00 per month if balance falls below $5,000
- **Minimum Opening Deposit:** $5,000
- **Interest Bearing:** No
- **Debit Card:** Visa Signature debit card with higher daily purchase and ATM limits
- **Mobile Banking:** Full mobile banking and mobile check deposit included
- **Online Bill Pay:** Included at no additional charge
- **Check Writing:** First 2 boxes per year free; additional boxes $25 each
- **ATM Access:** Free at all Solo Bank ATMs; up to $20/month in out-of-network domestic ATM fee rebates
- **Dedicated Phone Line:** Priority access to customer service with average hold time under 2 minutes
- **Cashier's Checks:** Free (no limit)
- **Safe Deposit Box Discount:** 25% off annual safe deposit box rental
- **Overdraft Options:** Eligible for all overdraft options with higher Standard Overdraft coverage limit ($1,000 vs. $500 for Basic)
- **Paper Statements:** Free; electronic statements also available
- **Free Notary:** Included
- **Eligibility:** Available to all customers who pass KYC/AML verification and ChexSystems review. No minimum credit score required.

## Savings Accounts

### Basic Savings

- **Annual Percentage Yield (APY):** 0.50%
- **Monthly Maintenance Fee:** $5.00, waived with $300 minimum daily balance
- **Minimum Opening Deposit:** $1 (no minimum to open)
- **Minimum Balance for APY:** $1.00 (interest earned on any balance)
- **Interest Compounding:** Daily compounding, credited monthly
- **Withdrawal Limit:** 6 free withdrawals or transfers per statement cycle (per Regulation D guidelines). Each additional withdrawal incurs a $10 excessive transaction fee.
- **ATM Access:** Free at Solo Bank ATMs
- **Online/Mobile Access:** Full access to view balance and initiate transfers
- **Overdraft Protection:** May be linked to a checking account for Overdraft Protection ($12 per transfer)
- **Eligibility:** Available to all customers. No minimum credit score. Minors may open with a custodial joint account holder.

### High-Yield Savings

- **Annual Percentage Yield (APY):** 4.25%
- **Monthly Maintenance Fee:** $0 (no monthly fee)
- **Minimum Opening Deposit:** $1,000
- **Minimum Balance for APY:** $1,000 (if balance falls below $1,000, APY drops to the Basic Savings rate of 0.50%)
- **Interest Compounding:** Daily compounding, credited monthly
- **Withdrawal Limit:** 6 free withdrawals or transfers per statement cycle. Each additional withdrawal incurs a $10 excessive transaction fee.
- **ATM Access:** Free at Solo Bank ATMs
- **Online/Mobile Access:** Full access to view balance and initiate transfers
- **Overdraft Protection:** May be linked to a checking account for Overdraft Protection ($12 per transfer)
- **Eligibility:** Available to all customers. No minimum credit score required.

### Money Market Account

- **Annual Percentage Yield (APY):** 4.50%
- **Monthly Maintenance Fee:** $0 (no monthly fee)
- **Minimum Opening Deposit:** $10,000
- **Minimum Balance for APY:** $10,000 (if balance falls below $10,000, APY drops to 0.50%)
- **Interest Compounding:** Daily compounding, credited monthly
- **Tiered Rates:**
  - $10,000–$49,999: 4.50% APY
  - $50,000–$99,999: 4.55% APY
  - $100,000+: 4.60% APY
- **Check Writing:** Limited check-writing privileges (3 checks per statement cycle)
- **Withdrawal Limit:** 6 free withdrawals or transfers per statement cycle
- **ATM Access:** Free at Solo Bank ATMs; Visa debit card available for ATM withdrawals
- **Overdraft Protection:** May be linked to a checking account for Overdraft Protection ($12 per transfer)
- **Eligibility:** Available to all customers. No minimum credit score required.

## Certificates of Deposit (CDs)

Solo Bank offers CDs with fixed rates and terms ranging from 3 months to 5 years. See [CD Rates](../rates/cd-rates.md) for current rates.

- **Minimum Deposit:** $1,000 for all CD terms
- **Interest Compounding:** Daily compounding, credited monthly (or at maturity for terms under 6 months)
- **Interest Payment Options:** Credited to the CD, transferred to a Solo Bank deposit account, or mailed by check
- **Automatic Renewal:** CDs automatically renew at maturity for the same term at the then-current rate unless the customer instructs otherwise during the 10-day grace period
- **Early Withdrawal Penalties:**
  - Terms under 12 months: 90 days of interest
  - Terms 12–35 months: 180 days of interest
  - Terms 36–59 months: 270 days of interest
  - Terms 60 months: 365 days of interest
- **FDIC Insurance:** All deposits are insured up to $250,000 per depositor per ownership category

## Account Ownership Types

All account types above are available in the following ownership structures:

- **Individual:** Single owner
- **Joint:** Two or more owners with rights of survivorship
- **Custodial (UTMA/UGMA):** Adult custodian for a minor beneficiary
- **Trust:** Revocable or irrevocable trust accounts
- **Business:** Sole proprietorship, LLC, corporation, partnership, or non-profit (subject to additional documentation requirements)

## FDIC Insurance

All Solo Bank deposit accounts are insured by the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC) up to $250,000 per depositor, per ownership category. Customers with deposits exceeding $250,000 should consult with a banker about structuring accounts to maximize insurance coverage.
