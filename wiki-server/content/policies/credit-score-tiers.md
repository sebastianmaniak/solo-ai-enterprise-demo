# Credit Score Tier System

**Policy ID:** POL-CS-001
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Revised:** March 15, 2026

## Overview

Solo Bank uses a five-tier credit score classification system based on FICO scores to determine product eligibility, interest rate adjustments, credit limits, and approval authority levels. All lending decisions, credit card applications, and line-of-credit determinations reference this tier system.

## Tier Definitions

### Tier 1 — Excellent (800–850)

- **Rate Adjustment:** Base Rate + 0.000% (no adjustment)
- **Auto-Approve Limit:** Up to $750,000 for qualifying products
- **Credit Limit Formula:** Up to 50% of verified gross annual salary
- **Approval Authority:** Automated approval for amounts within auto-approve limit
- **Eligible Products:** All Solo Bank products including Platinum Rewards Card, Jumbo Mortgages, and Home Equity Lines of Credit

### Tier 2 — Very Good (740–799)

- **Rate Adjustment:** Base Rate + 0.250%
- **Auto-Approve Limit:** Up to $500,000 for qualifying products
- **Credit Limit Formula:** Up to 40% of verified gross annual salary
- **Approval Authority:** Automated approval for amounts within auto-approve limit
- **Eligible Products:** All Solo Bank products including Platinum Rewards Card, Jumbo Mortgages, and Home Equity Lines of Credit

### Tier 3 — Good (670–739)

- **Rate Adjustment:** Base Rate + 0.750%
- **Auto-Approve Limit:** Up to $300,000 for qualifying products
- **Credit Limit Formula:** Up to 30% of verified gross annual salary
- **Approval Authority:** Automated approval for amounts within auto-approve limit
- **Eligible Products:** Conventional mortgages, Cashback Card, Basic and Premium accounts, Home Equity Lines of Credit (with additional underwriting)

### Tier 4 — Fair (580–669)

- **Rate Adjustment:** Base Rate + 1.500%
- **Auto-Approve Limit:** None — all amounts require Branch Manager approval
- **Credit Limit Formula:** Up to 20% of verified gross annual salary, hard cap of $10,000
- **Approval Authority:** Branch Manager must sign off on every application
- **Eligible Products:** Conventional mortgages (10% minimum down), Secured Card, Basic Checking, Basic Savings

### Tier 5 — Poor (300–579)

- **Rate Adjustment:** Base Rate + 3.000%
- **Auto-Approve Limit:** None — all amounts require Senior Manager approval
- **Credit Limit Formula:** Deposit-based limits only (credit limit equals secured deposit amount)
- **Approval Authority:** Senior Manager or Regional Credit Officer must approve
- **Eligible Products:** Secured Card only, Basic Checking, Basic Savings. Not eligible for mortgage products.

## Relationship Discounts

Long-standing customer relationships qualify for rate discounts applied on top of the tier-based rate adjustment:

| Relationship Duration | Rate Discount |
|---|---|
| Less than 5 years | No discount |
| 5–9 years | -0.125% |
| 10+ years | -0.250% |

Relationship duration is measured from the date the customer's oldest active account was opened. Closed accounts do not count toward duration. The discount applies to all new lending products and renewals.

## Tier Determination Process

1. Pull FICO score from all three bureaus (Equifax, Experian, TransUnion) at time of application.
2. Use the **middle score** of the three bureau scores. If only two scores are available, use the lower of the two.
3. Assign the tier based on the score ranges defined above.
4. Verify relationship duration from the core banking system for discount eligibility.
5. Document the tier assignment and any applicable discounts in the loan file.

## Score Rechecks

- Credit scores are pulled at the time of each new application.
- For existing revolving credit products, scores are reviewed quarterly.
- If a customer's score improves to a higher tier, they may request a product upgrade or rate reduction. The new tier takes effect on the next billing cycle after approval.
- If a customer's score drops to a lower tier, existing products remain at their current terms until renewal, at which point the new tier applies.

## Exceptions

Any exception to the tier-based pricing or eligibility rules must be documented on Form EX-100 and approved by the Regional Credit Officer. Exceptions for Tier 5 customers require VP-level approval. All exceptions are subject to quarterly audit review.
