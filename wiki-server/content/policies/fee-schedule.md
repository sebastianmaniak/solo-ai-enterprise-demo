# Fee Schedule

**Policy ID:** POL-FS-007
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Revised:** March 1, 2026

## Overview

This document is the comprehensive fee schedule for all Solo Bank consumer banking products and services. All fees are assessed in U.S. dollars. Fees are subject to change with 30 days advance notice to affected customers. See individual product documentation for detailed terms.

## Account Maintenance Fees

| Account Type | Monthly Fee | Waiver Conditions |
|---|---|---|
| Basic Checking | $12.00 | Waived with $1,500 minimum daily balance OR qualifying direct deposit of $500+/month |
| Premium Checking | $0.00 | No fee with $5,000 minimum daily balance; $25.00/month if balance falls below $5,000 |
| Basic Savings | $5.00 | Waived with $300 minimum daily balance |
| High-Yield Savings | $0.00 | No monthly fee (requires $1,000 minimum balance to open) |
| Money Market | $0.00 | No monthly fee (requires $10,000 minimum balance to open) |

## Fee Waivers and Adjustments

Customer Service representatives may waive certain fees subject to the authority levels defined in the [Customer Service Escalation Policy](customer-service-escalation.md):

- **Level 1 (L1):** May waive up to $100 in fees per customer per calendar year
- **Level 2 (L2):** May waive up to $500 in fees per customer per calendar year
- **Level 3 (L3):** Unlimited fee waiver authority

All fee waivers must be documented with a reason code in the core banking system.
