# Secured Card

**Product Code:** CC-SEC-003
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026

## Product Summary

The Solo Bank Secured Card is a credit-building card available to customers of all credit tiers, including those with limited or damaged credit history. The card requires a refundable security deposit that serves as the credit limit. With responsible use, cardholders can build or rebuild their credit and may qualify for an upgrade to an unsecured card.

## Eligibility

- **Credit Tier Required:** All tiers (Tier 1 through Tier 5)
- **Minimum Age:** 18 years old
- **Identification:** Must pass standard KYC verification (see [KYC/AML Compliance](../policies/kyc-aml-compliance.md))
- **ChexSystems:** Must not have an unresolved negative record in ChexSystems for fraud
- **Bankruptcy:** Applicants with an open (undischarged) bankruptcy are not eligible. Discharged bankruptcies are accepted.
- **Approval Authority:**
  - Tier 1–3: Automated approval
  - Tier 4: Branch Manager approval required
  - Tier 5: Senior Manager approval required

## Annual Fee

- **$39 per year**
- The annual fee is charged on the first statement and on each anniversary thereafter.
- The annual fee is deducted from the available credit limit (not from the security deposit).

## Security Deposit

- **Minimum Deposit:** $200
- **Maximum Deposit:** $5,000
- **Credit Limit:** Equals the security deposit amount (1:1 ratio)
- **Deposit Method:** Funds may be deposited from a Solo Bank account, by debit card, or by certified check at the time of application.
- **Deposit Account:** The security deposit is held in a Solo Bank non-interest-bearing custodial account.
- **Deposit Refund:** The full deposit is refunded when:
  - The account is closed in good standing with a $0 balance, OR
  - The account is upgraded to an unsecured card (see Upgrade Path below)
- **Partial Deposit Increase:** Cardholders may add to their security deposit at any time (in $100 increments) to increase their credit limit, up to the $5,000 maximum.

- **1% cashback** on all purchases
- Cashback is applied as a statement credit automatically each month when the total reaches $5 or more, or upon account closure/upgrade.
- There is no cap on cashback earned.

## Upgrade Path

The Secured Card is designed as a stepping stone to an unsecured credit card. The upgrade review process works as follows:

### Automatic Review

- After **12 consecutive months** of on-time payments (no payments more than 30 days past due), the account is automatically reviewed for upgrade eligibility.
- The review includes a soft credit pull (no impact to the customer's credit score).

### Upgrade Criteria

To qualify for an upgrade to the Cashback Card (unsecured):

1. **12 consecutive on-time payments** — no missed or late payments during the review period.
2. **Credit score improvement** to **Tier 3 (670) or above** at the time of review.
3. **No overlimit occurrences** during the review period.
4. **Account in good standing** — not currently in collections, dispute, or under fraud investigation.

### Upgrade Process

- If the customer meets all upgrade criteria, Solo Bank sends a notification offering the upgrade.
- The customer may accept or decline the upgrade.
- If accepted:
  - The Secured Card is closed and the Cashback Card is opened.
  - The security deposit is refunded in full within 10 business days (via check or transfer to a Solo Bank deposit account).
  - The credit limit on the new Cashback Card is set based on the Tier 3 (or higher) credit limit formula (up to 30% of verified gross annual salary).
  - The $39 annual fee is pro-rated for any remaining months and refunded.
  - The credit bureau reporting shows the original account as closed in good standing and the new account as a new tradeline.

### If Upgrade Criteria Are Not Met

- If the customer does not meet the upgrade criteria after 12 months, the account remains a Secured Card.
- A new automatic review occurs every 6 months thereafter.
- The customer is notified of the reason the upgrade was not approved and given guidance on steps to improve eligibility.

## Card Features

- **Credit Bureau Reporting:** Monthly reporting to all three major credit bureaus (Equifax, Experian, TransUnion). This is the primary benefit for credit building — on-time payments are reported as positive tradeline history.
- **Free FICO Score:** Monthly FICO score available through online banking and on statements.
- **Mobile Banking:** Full access to the Solo Bank mobile app for balance checks, payments, and alerts.
- **Contactless Payment:** Tap-to-pay enabled.
- **Fraud Alerts:** Real-time SMS and push notification fraud alerts.
- **Card Lock/Unlock:** Instantly lock or unlock the card via the mobile app.

## Important Notes

- The Secured Card does **not** offer balance transfer functionality.
- Cash advance limit is 30% of the total credit limit (which equals 30% of the security deposit).
- If the account is closed with an outstanding balance, the balance is deducted from the security deposit. Any remaining deposit is refunded.
- If the outstanding balance exceeds the security deposit (due to fees and interest), the cardholder is responsible for the difference.

## How to Apply

1. Apply online at solobank.com/secured, at any Solo Bank branch, or by calling 1-800-SOLO-BANK.
2. Provide the security deposit at the time of application.
3. Approval is typically processed within 1–2 business days.
4. The physical card arrives within 7–10 business days after approval and deposit confirmation.
