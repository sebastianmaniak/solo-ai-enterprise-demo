# Cashback Card

**Product Code:** CC-CASH-002
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026

## Product Summary

The Solo Bank Cashback Card is a no-annual-fee credit card offering unlimited 2% cashback on every purchase. It is designed for customers who prefer straightforward, no-hassle rewards without the complexity of points categories or rotating bonus structures.

## Eligibility

- **Credit Tier Required:** Tier 1 (800–850), Tier 2 (740–799), or Tier 3 (670–739)
- **Minimum Income:** No minimum income requirement
- **Credit Limit Range:**
  - Tier 1: Up to 50% of gross annual salary
  - Tier 2: Up to 40% of gross annual salary
  - Tier 3: Up to 30% of gross annual salary
- **Minimum Credit Limit:** $2,000

## Annual Fee

- **$0** — No annual fee, ever.
- Authorized user cards: $0 additional fee (up to 4 authorized users).

## Cashback Rewards

### Earning Rate

- **2% unlimited cashback** on every purchase, with no caps, no rotating categories, and no enrollment required.
- Cashback is earned on net purchases (purchases minus returns and credits).

### Cashback Redemption

- **Statement Credit:** Apply cashback as a credit to the account balance (any amount, no minimum).
- **Direct Deposit:** Transfer cashback to any Solo Bank checking or savings account.
- **Check:** Request a cashback check mailed to the address on file (minimum $25).
- Cashback is available for redemption as soon as it is posted (typically within 1–2 billing cycles after the qualifying purchase).

### Cashback Expiration

- Cashback does not expire as long as the account remains open and in good standing.
- If the account is closed, unredeemed cashback is forfeited 60 days after closure.

## Sign-Up Bonus

Earn a **$200 statement credit** after spending $1,000 on purchases within the first 3 months of account opening.

- The bonus is available to new cardholders only. Customers who have held a Solo Bank Cashback Card within the prior 24 months are not eligible.

## Promotional Offers

### New Account: 0% Intro APR on Purchases (Tier 1 Only)

- 0% APR on purchases for the first **6 months** from account opening.
- After the promotional period, the standard purchase APR applies.

### New Account: 0% Intro APR on Balance Transfers (Tier 1 and Tier 2)

- 0% APR on balance transfers for **15 months** from account opening.
- Balance transfer fee of 3% (minimum $5) applies.
- Balance transfers must be completed within 60 days of account opening.
- After the promotional period, the standard purchase APR applies to remaining balances.

## Card Features

### No Foreign Transaction Fees

- 0% fee on all purchases made outside the United States or in a foreign currency.

### Cell Phone Protection

- Up to **$600** per claim (2 claims per 12-month period, $50 deductible) when the monthly cell phone bill is paid with the Cashback Card.
- Covers theft and damage to the cardholder's cell phone.

### Free FICO Score

- Monthly FICO score available through online banking and on monthly statements.

### Contactless Payment

- Tap-to-pay enabled for fast, secure in-store payments.

### Virtual Card Numbers

- Generate disposable card numbers for online shopping to protect the physical card number.

### Fraud Protection

- Zero liability for unauthorized transactions reported promptly.
- Real-time fraud alerts via SMS and push notification.
- Instant card lock/unlock through the Solo Bank mobile app.

## How to Apply

1. Apply online at solobank.com/cashback, at any Solo Bank branch, or by calling 1-800-SOLO-BANK.
2. Approval decision is typically provided within 60 seconds for Tier 1–3 applicants within auto-approve limits.
3. If approved, a temporary card number is available immediately for online purchases.
4. The physical card arrives within 7–10 business days.
