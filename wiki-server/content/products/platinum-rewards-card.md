# Platinum Rewards Card

**Product Code:** CC-PLAT-001
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026

## Product Summary

The Solo Bank Platinum Rewards Card is our premium credit card offering, designed for customers with excellent credit who want to maximize rewards on dining, travel, and everyday spending. This card includes a comprehensive suite of travel benefits, purchase protections, and an industry-leading rewards program.

## Eligibility

- **Credit Tier Required:** Tier 1 (800–850) or Tier 2 (740–799)
- **Minimum Income:** No minimum income requirement, but credit limits are based on 50% of salary (Tier 1) or 40% of salary (Tier 2)
- **Minimum Credit Limit:** $10,000
- **Existing Relationship:** Not required, but relationship discounts may enhance other product offerings

## Annual Fee

- **$195 per year**
- The annual fee is charged on the first statement after account opening and on each anniversary date thereafter.
- The annual fee is non-refundable after 30 days of account opening.
- Authorized user cards: $0 additional fee (up to 5 authorized users).

## Rewards Program

### Earning Rates

| Category | Points per $1 Spent |
|---|---|
| Dining (restaurants, bars, cafes) | 3x |
| Travel (airlines, hotels, car rentals, ride-share) | 3x |
| Groceries (supermarkets, not warehouse clubs) | 2x |
| All other purchases | 1x |

### Points Redemption

- **Travel Redemption:** 1 point = $0.015 when redeemed through the Solo Bank Travel Portal (50% bonus value)
- **Statement Credit:** 1 point = $0.01
- **Gift Cards:** 1 point = $0.01
- **Cash Deposit to Solo Bank Account:** 1 point = $0.01
- **Transfer to Airline/Hotel Partners:** 1 point = 1 mile/point (12 airline and 8 hotel partners)

### Points Expiration

- Points do not expire as long as the account remains open and in good standing.
- If the account is closed or converted to a different product, unredeemed points expire after 60 days.

## Sign-Up Bonus

Earn **80,000 bonus points** after spending $4,000 on purchases within the first 3 months of account opening. This bonus is worth up to $1,200 when redeemed for travel through the Solo Bank Travel Portal.

- The sign-up bonus is available to new cardholders only. Customers who have held a Platinum Rewards Card within the prior 24 months are not eligible.

## Travel Benefits

### Airport Lounge Access

- Complimentary access to the Solo Lounges network (200+ lounges worldwide).
- Primary cardholder plus 2 guests per visit at no additional charge.

### Travel Accident Insurance

- Up to **$500,000** in coverage for accidental death or dismemberment when the full fare for a common carrier (airline, train, bus, cruise) is charged to the card.

### Trip Cancellation/Interruption Insurance

- Up to **$5,000** per trip, **$20,000** per year for non-refundable trip expenses when the trip is canceled or interrupted due to covered reasons (illness, severe weather, jury duty, etc.).

### Primary Rental Car Insurance

- Primary collision damage waiver (CDW) coverage for rental cars in the U.S. and abroad.
- Covers damage or theft up to the actual cash value of the vehicle.
- This is primary coverage, meaning it pays before your personal auto insurance.

### No Foreign Transaction Fees

- 0% foreign transaction fee on all purchases made outside the United States.

### Baggage Delay Insurance

- Up to $500 in coverage for essential purchases when checked baggage is delayed more than 6 hours.

## Purchase Protections

### Purchase Protection

- Coverage for eligible items purchased with the card against damage or theft for **120 days** from the purchase date.
- Maximum coverage: $10,000 per claim, $50,000 per year.

### Extended Warranty

- Extends the manufacturer's warranty by an additional **1 year** (on warranties of 3 years or less).
- Maximum coverage: $10,000 per claim.

### Return Protection

- If a merchant will not accept a return within 90 days of purchase, Solo Bank will refund up to $500 per item, $1,000 per year.

## Additional Features

- **Free FICO Score:** Monthly FICO score available on statements and online banking.
- **Virtual Card Numbers:** Generate virtual card numbers for online purchases to protect your actual card number.
- **Contactless Payment:** Tap-to-pay enabled.
- **Cell Phone Protection:** Up to $800 per claim (2 claims per year, $50 deductible) when the monthly cell phone bill is paid with the card.
- **Concierge Service:** 24/7 concierge service for travel bookings, restaurant reservations, and event tickets.

## How to Apply

1. Apply online at solobank.com/platinum, at any Solo Bank branch, or by calling 1-800-SOLO-BANK.
2. Approval decision is typically provided within 60 seconds for Tier 1 and Tier 2 applicants.
3. If approved, a temporary card number is issued immediately for online purchases.
4. The physical card arrives within 7–10 business days (rush delivery available for $25).
