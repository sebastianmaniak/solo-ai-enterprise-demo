# High-Yield Savings Account

**Product Code:** DEP-HYS-006
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026

## Product Summary

The Solo Bank High-Yield Savings account offers a highly competitive 4.25% APY on balances of $1,000 or more, with no monthly maintenance fee. It is designed for customers who want to grow their savings while maintaining easy access to their funds.

## Eligibility

- **Credit Tier Required:** None — available to all customers regardless of credit score
- **Minimum Age:** 18 years old (16 with a joint custodial account holder)
- **KYC/AML Verification:** Standard identity verification required
- **Existing Account Requirement:** None — the High-Yield Savings account can be opened as a standalone account or alongside any other Solo Bank product

## Annual Percentage Yield (APY)

- **4.25% APY** on balances of $1,000 or more
- If the balance falls below $1,000 on any day, the rate for that day reverts to the Basic Savings rate of **0.50% APY**
- The APY is variable and may change at any time. Rate changes are announced on the Solo Bank website and take effect immediately.
- See [Savings Rates](../rates/savings-rates.md) for rate history and comparison to other deposit products.

### Interest Calculation

- Interest is compounded **daily** and credited to the account on the last business day of each **month**.
- Interest is calculated using the daily balance method:
  - Daily rate = APY / 365
  - Daily interest = balance x daily rate
  - Monthly interest = sum of daily interest amounts for the statement period

### Example

A customer maintains a balance of $15,000 for an entire month (30 days) at 4.25% APY:
- Daily rate: 4.25% / 365 = 0.01164%
- Daily interest: $15,000 x 0.0001164 = $1.75
- Monthly interest: $1.75 x 30 = **$52.40**
- Annual interest at this balance: approximately **$637.50**

## Fees

- **Monthly Maintenance Fee:** $0 — no monthly fee regardless of balance
- **Excessive Withdrawal Fee:** $10 per withdrawal or transfer beyond the 6-free-per-cycle limit
- **Account Closure Fee:** None
- **Dormant Account Fee:** $10/month after 12 months of no activity

## Minimum Balances

- **Minimum to Open:** $1,000
- **Minimum for APY:** $1,000 (balances below this earn 0.50% APY)
- **Minimum to Avoid Fees:** No minimum — there is no monthly fee to waive

## Withdrawal Limits

- **6 free withdrawals or transfers per statement cycle** (consistent with standard savings account practice)
- Withdrawals include: outgoing transfers (internal and external), ACH debits, wire transfers, and check payments (if applicable)
- Withdrawals that **do not count** toward the limit: ATM withdrawals, in-person branch withdrawals, incoming transfers
- Each withdrawal beyond 6 per cycle incurs a **$10 excessive transaction fee**
- If excessive withdrawals occur in 3 consecutive statement cycles, Solo Bank may convert the account to a Basic Checking account with notice

## Access Methods

### ATM Access

- Free withdrawals at all Solo Bank ATMs (does not count toward the 6-withdrawal limit)
- ATM deposits accepted at Solo Bank ATMs with immediate availability for cash, next business day for checks

### Online and Mobile Banking

- Full account visibility through the Solo Bank mobile app and online banking portal
- Initiate transfers to/from linked Solo Bank accounts (internal transfers are immediate)
- Initiate transfers to/from external bank accounts (ACH transfers take 1–3 business days)
- View interest earned (month-to-date and year-to-date)
- Download statements and tax forms (1099-INT)

### Branch Access

- Deposits and withdrawals available at any Solo Bank branch
- In-person withdrawals do not count toward the 6-per-cycle limit

## Overdraft Protection

The High-Yield Savings account can be linked to a Solo Bank checking account (Basic or Premium) as the funding source for **Overdraft Protection**.

- When the linked checking account has insufficient funds, the bank automatically transfers money from the High-Yield Savings account.
- Transfer fee: **$12 per transfer**
- Transfers are made in **$100 increments**, covering the shortfall rounded up to the nearest $100.
- The savings account must maintain a minimum balance of $25 after the transfer.
- Overdraft Protection transfers **do count** toward the 6-per-cycle withdrawal limit.

## FDIC Insurance

Deposits in the High-Yield Savings account are insured by the FDIC up to **$250,000 per depositor, per ownership category**. Joint accounts are insured up to $250,000 per co-owner.

## How to Open

1. Visit any Solo Bank branch with a government-issued photo ID, Social Security Number, and $1,000 for the opening deposit.
2. Or apply online at solobank.com/high-yield-savings and fund via:
   - ACH transfer from an external bank (1–3 business days)
   - Transfer from an existing Solo Bank account (immediate)
   - Wire transfer (same-day)
3. The account begins earning interest on the day the opening deposit is credited.
4. Online banking access is available immediately after account opening.
