# Basic Checking Account

**Product Code:** DEP-BCK-005
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026

## Product Summary

Solo Bank Basic Checking is an everyday checking account with a low barrier to entry, providing essential banking services including a debit card, mobile banking, online bill pay, and mobile check deposit. The monthly fee is easily waived with a modest minimum balance or qualifying direct deposit.

## Eligibility

- **Credit Tier Required:** None — available to all customers regardless of credit score
- **Minimum Age:** 18 years old (16 with a joint custodial account holder)
- **KYC/AML Verification:** Standard identity verification required (see [New Account Opening](../procedures/new-account-opening.md))
- **ChexSystems:** Account opening is subject to ChexSystems review. Customers with a negative ChexSystems record within the past 5 years may be declined.

## Fees

- **Monthly Maintenance Fee:** $12.00 per month
- **Fee Waiver Conditions** (either one qualifies):
  - Maintain a **$1,500 minimum daily balance** throughout the statement cycle, OR
  - Receive a qualifying **direct deposit of $500 or more** per month (payroll, government benefits, or pension)
- **Account Closure Fee:** $25 if the account is closed within 90 days of opening
- No per-transaction fees for debit card purchases or ACH transfers

## Minimum Opening Deposit

- **$25** required at account opening.

## Interest

- Basic Checking is a **non-interest-bearing** account.
- For interest-bearing options, see [Basic Savings](basic-savings.md) or [High-Yield Savings](high-yield-savings.md).

## Debit Card

- **Visa Debit Card** included at no charge.
- Daily ATM withdrawal limit: $500
- Daily purchase limit: $5,000
- Contactless (tap-to-pay) enabled
- Instant card lock/unlock via the Solo Bank mobile app
- Replacement card (standard delivery 7–10 days): $5.00
- Replacement card (rush delivery 2–3 days): $25.00
- Temporary debit card available at branches: $5.00

## ATM Access

- **Free at all Solo Bank ATMs** (500+ nationwide)
- **Out-of-network domestic ATM:** $3.00 per transaction (charged by Solo Bank; the ATM operator may also charge a separate surcharge)
- **International ATM:** $5.00 per transaction
- **Balance inquiry at out-of-network ATM:** $1.00

## Check Writing

- Standard check order: **$25 per box**
- Cashier's check: **$10.00** per check
- Counter checks available at branches: $2.00 per check

## Mobile and Online Banking

- **Mobile Banking App:** Full-featured app for iOS and Android:
  - Mobile check deposit (limit: $2,500 per day, $10,000 per month)
  - Account balance and transaction history
  - Internal and external fund transfers
  - Bill pay at no additional charge
  - Card management (lock/unlock, travel notifications, PIN change)
  - ATM/branch locator
- **Online Banking:** Desktop access with statement downloads, tax documents, and account management
- **Zelle:** Person-to-person payments through Zelle, integrated into mobile and online banking

## Overdraft Options

Basic Checking is eligible for all three overdraft coverage options:

1. **Standard Overdraft Service:** Coverage up to **$500**. Fee: $35 per item, maximum 3 per day ($105 max). Opt-in required for ATM and one-time debit card transactions per Regulation E.
2. **Overdraft Protection (Linked Savings):** Link a Solo Bank savings account for automatic transfers. Fee: $12 per transfer, in $100 increments.
3. **Overdraft Line of Credit:** Variable rate (currently Prime + 8.00% = 16.50% APR). Requires separate credit application and approval.

See [Overdraft Policy](../policies/overdraft-policy.md) for full details including the de minimis threshold ($5) and grace period.

## Paper and Electronic Statements

- **Paper statements:** Mailed monthly at no charge.
- **Electronic statements:** Available through online banking and mobile app.
- Customers may opt for e-statements only.

## Account Alerts

- Customizable alerts for:
  - Low balance (configurable threshold)
  - Large transactions (configurable amount)
  - Deposits received
  - Direct deposit posted
  - Card transactions
- Delivered via email, SMS, or push notification.

## How to Open

1. Visit any Solo Bank branch with a government-issued photo ID, Social Security Number, and $25 for the opening deposit.
2. Or apply online at solobank.com/basic-checking and fund via ACH transfer, debit card, or wire.
3. Debit card arrives within 7–10 business days. A temporary card may be issued at the branch for $5.
4. Online banking credentials are set up during the account opening process.
5. Direct deposit form is provided to facilitate payroll setup for fee waiver.
