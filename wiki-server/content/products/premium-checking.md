# Premium Checking Account

**Product Code:** DEP-PCK-004
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026

## Product Summary

Solo Bank Premium Checking is our full-featured checking account for customers who maintain higher balances. It includes ATM fee rebates, free checks, a dedicated customer service phone line, safe deposit box discounts, and other premium benefits — all with no monthly fee when the minimum balance is maintained.

## Eligibility

- **Credit Tier Required:** None — available to all customers regardless of credit score
- **Minimum Age:** 18 years old (16 with a joint custodial account holder)
- **KYC/AML Verification:** Standard identity verification required (see [New Account Opening](../procedures/new-account-opening.md))
- **ChexSystems:** Account opening is subject to ChexSystems review. Customers with a negative ChexSystems record may be declined or offered Basic Checking instead.

## Fees

- **Monthly Maintenance Fee:** $0 with a **$5,000 minimum daily balance**
- If the daily balance falls below $5,000 on any day during the statement cycle, a **$25.00 monthly fee** is charged.
- **Account Closure Fee:** $25 if the account is closed within 90 days of opening.
- No per-check fees. No per-transaction fees.

## Minimum Opening Deposit

- **$5,000** required at account opening (cash, check, transfer, or wire).

## Interest

- Premium Checking is a **non-interest-bearing** account.
- Customers seeking interest on liquid funds should consider our [High-Yield Savings](high-yield-savings.md) or [Money Market](../policies/account-types.md) accounts, which can be linked for easy transfers.

## Debit Card

- **Visa Signature Debit Card** included at no charge.
- Daily ATM withdrawal limit: $1,000 (vs. $500 for Basic Checking)
- Daily purchase limit: $10,000 (vs. $5,000 for Basic Checking)
- Contactless (tap-to-pay) enabled.
- Instant card lock/unlock via the Solo Bank mobile app.

## ATM Access

- **Free at all Solo Bank ATMs** (nationwide network of 500+ ATMs)
- **Out-of-network domestic ATM rebates:** Up to **$20 per month** in out-of-network ATM fee refunds. Solo Bank's $3.00 per-transaction fee is waived, and ATM surcharges charged by the ATM operator are reimbursed up to $20/month total.
- **International ATM:** $5.00 per transaction (not included in the rebate program)

## Check Writing

- **First 2 boxes of standard checks per year:** Free
- Additional check orders: $25 per box
- **Cashier's checks:** Free, unlimited
- **Counter checks:** Available at branches at no charge

## Premium Benefits

### Dedicated Customer Service Line

- Priority phone line with an average hold time of **under 2 minutes**.
- Available Monday–Friday 7 AM – 10 PM ET, Saturday 8 AM – 6 PM ET.
- Dedicated team trained on Premium account features and escalation paths.

### Safe Deposit Box Discount

- **25% off** the annual rental fee for any safe deposit box size at any Solo Bank branch.
- Small (3"x5"): $50 → **$37.50**
- Medium (5"x10"): $100 → **$75.00**
- Large (10"x10"): $175 → **$131.25**

### Notary Service

- Free notary service at any Solo Bank branch (by appointment).

### Wire Transfer Discounts

- Domestic outgoing wire transfers: $25 (standard pricing)
- International outgoing wire transfers: $45 (standard pricing)
- Premium Checking customers receive **2 free domestic wire transfers per calendar year**.

### Free Account Research

- First hour of account research per year is free ($25/hour thereafter).

## Mobile and Online Banking

- **Mobile Banking App:** Full-featured app for iOS and Android, including:
  - Mobile check deposit (up to $10,000/day for Premium, vs. $2,500/day for Basic)
  - Account balance and transaction history
  - Internal and external transfers
  - Bill pay
  - Card management (lock/unlock, travel notifications, PIN change)
  - Budgeting tools and spending insights
- **Online Banking:** Full desktop experience with all mobile features plus detailed statement downloads and tax document access.

## Overdraft Options

Premium Checking is eligible for all three overdraft coverage options:

1. **Standard Overdraft Service:** Coverage up to **$1,000** (vs. $500 for Basic Checking). Fee: $35 per item, max 3 per day. Requires opt-in for ATM/debit transactions.
2. **Overdraft Protection (Linked Savings):** $12 per transfer from a linked Solo Bank savings account.
3. **Overdraft Line of Credit:** Variable rate (currently Prime + 8.00%). Must apply separately.

See [Overdraft Policy](../policies/overdraft-policy.md) for full details.

## Paper and Electronic Statements

- **Paper statements:** Mailed monthly at no charge.
- **Electronic statements:** Available through online banking and the mobile app.
- Customers may opt for e-statements only to reduce paper.

## Account Alerts

- Customizable alerts for low balance, large transactions, deposits, and withdrawals.
- Delivered via email, SMS, or push notification.

## How to Open

1. Visit any Solo Bank branch with a government-issued photo ID, Social Security Number, and $5,000 for the opening deposit.
2. Or apply online at solobank.com/premium-checking and fund via ACH transfer or wire.
3. Debit card arrives within 7–10 business days. A temporary card may be issued at the branch.
4. Online banking credentials are set up during the account opening process.
