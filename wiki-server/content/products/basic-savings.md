# Basic Savings Account

**Product Code:** DEP-BSV-007
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026

## Product Summary

The Solo Bank Basic Savings account is an accessible entry-level savings product with no minimum deposit requirement to open. It offers a modest interest rate and is ideal for customers starting to build their savings, setting aside an emergency fund, or maintaining a linked account for overdraft protection.

## Eligibility

- **Credit Tier Required:** None — available to all customers regardless of credit score
- **Minimum Age:** 18 years old (16 with a joint custodial account holder; custodial accounts for minors of any age are also available under UTMA/UGMA)
- **KYC/AML Verification:** Standard identity verification required
- **Existing Account Requirement:** None — can be opened as a standalone account

## Annual Percentage Yield (APY)

- **0.50% APY** on all balances
- No tiered rates — the same 0.50% APY applies regardless of balance
- Minimum balance to earn interest: **$1.00**
- The APY is variable and may change at any time
- See [Savings Rates](../rates/savings-rates.md) for current rates and history

### Interest Calculation

- Interest is compounded **daily** and credited on the last business day of each **month**
- Calculated using the daily balance method:
  - Daily rate = APY / 365
  - Daily interest = closing balance x daily rate

### Example

A customer maintains a balance of $2,000 for an entire month (30 days) at 0.50% APY:
- Daily rate: 0.50% / 365 = 0.00137%
- Daily interest: $2,000 x 0.0000137 = $0.027
- Monthly interest: $0.027 x 30 = **$0.82**
- Annual interest at this balance: approximately **$10.00**

## Fees

- **Monthly Maintenance Fee:** $5.00 per month
- **Fee Waiver:** Monthly fee is waived when the account maintains a **$300 minimum daily balance** throughout the statement cycle
- **Excessive Withdrawal Fee:** $10 per withdrawal or transfer beyond the 6-free-per-cycle limit
- **Account Closure Fee:** None
- **Dormant Account Fee:** $10/month after 12 months with no customer-initiated activity

## Minimum Balances

- **Minimum to Open:** $0 (no minimum opening deposit; however, the account must be funded within 30 days or it may be closed)
- **Minimum to Earn Interest:** $1.00
- **Minimum to Waive Monthly Fee:** $300 daily balance

## Withdrawal Limits

- **6 free withdrawals or transfers per statement cycle**
- Covered transactions: outgoing transfers (internal and external), ACH debits, wire transfers, telephone-initiated transfers
- **Not counted** toward the limit: ATM withdrawals, in-person branch withdrawals
- Each withdrawal beyond 6 per cycle: **$10 excessive transaction fee**
- Repeated excessive withdrawal activity (3 consecutive cycles) may result in account conversion to Basic Checking with notice

## Access Methods

### ATM Access

- Free withdrawals at all Solo Bank ATMs
- ATM deposits accepted at Solo Bank ATMs
- No debit card is provided with Basic Savings; ATM access requires a Solo Bank ATM card (provided upon request at no charge)

### Online and Mobile Banking

- Full account visibility through the Solo Bank mobile app and online banking
- Initiate internal transfers to/from linked Solo Bank accounts
- Initiate external ACH transfers to/from accounts at other banks (1–3 business days)
- View interest earned (month-to-date and year-to-date)
- Download monthly statements and annual tax forms (1099-INT)

### Branch Access

- Deposits and withdrawals available at any Solo Bank branch during business hours
- In-person withdrawals do not count toward the 6-per-cycle limit

## Overdraft Protection

The Basic Savings account can be linked to a Solo Bank checking account (Basic or Premium) to serve as the funding source for **Overdraft Protection**:

- When the linked checking account has insufficient funds, money is automatically transferred from the savings account
- Transfer fee: **$12 per transfer**
- Transfers are made in **$100 increments**, rounded up to cover the shortfall
- The savings account must maintain a minimum balance of **$25** after the transfer; if the balance is insufficient, the transfer will not occur
- Overdraft Protection transfers **count** toward the 6-per-cycle withdrawal limit

## Account Features

- **Automatic Savings Plan:** Set up recurring automatic transfers from a Solo Bank checking account on a weekly, biweekly, or monthly schedule. There is no fee for incoming automatic transfers.
- **Goal Tracking:** Use the Solo Bank mobile app to set savings goals and track progress. Multiple goals can be assigned within a single Basic Savings account using virtual "buckets."
- **Joint Account Option:** Two or more individuals can hold the account jointly with rights of survivorship.
- **UTMA/UGMA Custodial Accounts:** Adults may open custodial savings accounts for minors. The custodian manages the account until the minor reaches the age of majority (18 or 21 depending on state).

## FDIC Insurance

Deposits in the Basic Savings account are insured by the FDIC up to **$250,000 per depositor, per ownership category**.

## When to Consider Upgrading

Customers may benefit from upgrading to a **High-Yield Savings** account if:

- They can maintain a balance of **$1,000 or more** (required to earn the 4.25% APY)
- They want to earn significantly more interest (4.25% vs. 0.50%)
- They want to avoid any monthly maintenance fee regardless of balance

The High-Yield Savings account has no monthly fee but requires a $1,000 minimum to open and to earn the full APY. See [High-Yield Savings](high-yield-savings.md) for details.

## How to Open

1. Visit any Solo Bank branch with a government-issued photo ID and Social Security Number. No minimum deposit required.
2. Or apply online at solobank.com/basic-savings and fund via ACH transfer, internal transfer, or wire.
3. The account is active immediately upon opening. Interest begins accruing on the day the first deposit is credited.
4. Online and mobile banking access is available immediately.
