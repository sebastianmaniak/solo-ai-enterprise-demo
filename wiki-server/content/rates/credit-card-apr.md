# Credit Card APR Schedule

**Effective Date:** April 1, 2026
**Next Review:** May 1, 2026

## Overview

Solo Bank credit card Annual Percentage Rates (APR) vary by card product and the cardholder's credit tier at the time of application. APRs for the Platinum Rewards Card and Cashback Card are variable and tied to the U.S. Prime Rate. The Secured Card has a fixed APR. All rates are subject to the terms of the cardholder agreement.

## Current U.S. Prime Rate

As of April 1, 2026, the U.S. Prime Rate is **8.50%**.

## Purchase APR by Card and Tier

### Platinum Rewards Card

| Credit Tier | Score Range | Purchase APR |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 — Excellent | 800–850 | 18.99% variable |
| Tier 2 — Very Good | 740–799 | 20.99% variable |

Tiers 3, 4, and 5 are not eligible for the Platinum Rewards Card.

### Cashback Card

| Credit Tier | Score Range | Purchase APR |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 — Excellent | 800–850 | 19.99% variable |
| Tier 2 — Very Good | 740–799 | 21.99% variable |
| Tier 3 — Good | 670–739 | 24.99% variable |

Tiers 4 and 5 are not eligible for the Cashback Card.

### Secured Card

| Credit Tier | Score Range | Purchase APR |
|---|---|---|
| All Tiers (1–5) | 300–850 | 22.99% fixed |

The Secured Card APR is fixed and does not change with the Prime Rate.

## Cash Advance APR

Cash advance APR is calculated as the cardholder's purchase APR plus 4.00%:

| Card | Tier 1 | Tier 2 | Tier 3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Platinum Rewards | 22.99% | 24.99% | N/A |
| Cashback | 23.99% | 25.99% | 28.99% |
| Secured | 26.99% | 26.99% | 26.99% |

- Cash advances do not have a grace period. Interest accrues from the date of the transaction.
- Cash advance fee: 5% of the amount, minimum $10.

## Balance Transfer APR

| Card | Standard Balance Transfer APR | Promotional APR (New Accounts) |
|---|---|---|
| Platinum Rewards | Same as Purchase APR | 0% for 15 months (Tier 1 and Tier 2) |
| Cashback | Same as Purchase APR | 0% for 15 months (Tier 1 and Tier 2 only) |
| Secured | Not available | Not available |

- Balance transfer fee: 3% of the amount, minimum $5.
- After the promotional period ends, the standard purchase APR applies to the remaining balance.
- Balance transfers must be completed within 60 days of account opening to qualify for the promotional rate.

## Penalty APR

**Penalty APR: 29.99%** (applies to all cards, all tiers)

The Penalty APR may be applied to the account if:

- The minimum payment is **60 or more days past due**.
- A payment is returned (e.g., insufficient funds).

### Penalty APR Duration

- The Penalty APR applies to all existing and new balances on the account.
- The Penalty APR will be reviewed after **6 consecutive months** of on-time payments. If all payments during that period are received on time and for at least the minimum amount, the Penalty APR will be removed and the account will revert to the standard purchase APR.
- If the cardholder is delinquent again within the 6-month review period, the clock resets.

## Promotional APR Offers

### New Account Promotional Rates

| Offer | Eligible Cards | Eligible Tiers | Promotional APR | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Purchase APR Promo | Platinum Rewards | Tier 1 | 0% | 12 months |
| Purchase APR Promo | Cashback | Tier 1 | 0% | 6 months |
| Balance Transfer Promo | Platinum Rewards | Tier 1, Tier 2 | 0% | 15 months |
| Balance Transfer Promo | Cashback | Tier 1, Tier 2 | 0% | 15 months |

- Promotional rates apply only to new accounts opened after the effective date above.
- If a payment is 60+ days late during the promotional period, the promotional rate is revoked and the Penalty APR of 29.99% applies immediately.

### Retention Promotional Rates

Existing cardholders who contact Solo Bank regarding account closure may be offered a promotional rate at the discretion of a Level 2 (L2) or Level 3 (L3) customer service representative:

- Typical retention offer: Reduced APR of 9.99% for 6 months on existing balances
- Retention offers are documented in the CRM and subject to approval per the [Customer Service Escalation Policy](../policies/customer-service-escalation.md)

## How Variable APR Is Calculated

Variable APRs are calculated as: **Prime Rate + Margin**

| Card | Tier | Margin | Current APR (Prime 8.50%) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Platinum Rewards | Tier 1 | 10.49% | 18.99% |
| Platinum Rewards | Tier 2 | 12.49% | 20.99% |
| Cashback | Tier 1 | 11.49% | 19.99% |
| Cashback | Tier 2 | 13.49% | 21.99% |
| Cashback | Tier 3 | 16.49% | 24.99% |

Variable APRs adjust on the first day of the billing cycle following a change in the Prime Rate.

## Minimum Interest Charge

If interest is charged for a billing period, the minimum interest charge is **$2.00**.

## Grace Period

- A grace period of at least **25 days** from the statement closing date applies to purchases, provided the previous statement balance was paid in full by the due date.
- No grace period applies to cash advances or balances subject to the Penalty APR.

## Important Disclosures

- APR information is provided in accordance with the Truth in Lending Act (TILA) and Regulation Z.
- The actual APR applied to a cardholder's account depends on the credit tier at the time of application and does not change if the cardholder's credit score subsequently changes (except as noted under Penalty APR).
- Variable rates will increase if the Prime Rate increases.
- All rates assume no promotional offer is active. Promotional rates supersede standard rates during the promotional period.
