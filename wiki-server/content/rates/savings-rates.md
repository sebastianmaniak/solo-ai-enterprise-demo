# Savings & Deposit Rates

**Effective Date:** April 1, 2026
**Next Review:** May 1, 2026

## Overview

The following Annual Percentage Yields (APY) apply to Solo Bank savings and deposit accounts. Interest is compounded daily and credited monthly unless otherwise noted. Rates are variable and may change at any time without notice, except for Certificate of Deposit (CD) rates which are fixed for the term. For CD rates, see [CD Rates](cd-rates.md).

## Interest Calculation Method

All savings and deposit account interest is calculated using the **daily balance method**:

1. The daily periodic rate is calculated as APY / 365 (or 366 in a leap year).
2. Each day's interest = daily closing balance x daily periodic rate.
3. Accrued daily interest is credited to the account on the last business day of each month.

### Example Calculation

A High-Yield Savings account with a constant balance of $25,000 at 4.25% APY:

- Daily periodic rate: 4.25% / 365 = 0.01164%
- Daily interest earned: $25,000 x 0.0001164 = $2.91
- Monthly interest (30 days): $2.91 x 30 = **$87.33**
- Annual interest earned: approximately **$1,062.50**

## Checking Account Interest

Solo Bank checking accounts are **non-interest-bearing**. Neither Basic Checking nor Premium Checking earns interest. Customers seeking interest on liquid funds should consider the High-Yield Savings or Money Market accounts.

## FDIC Insurance

All deposits at Solo Bank are insured by the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC) up to $250,000 per depositor, per ownership category. This includes savings accounts, money market accounts, checking accounts, and certificates of deposit.

## Important Disclosures

- APYs are accurate as of the effective date shown above.
- Fees may reduce earnings on all accounts.
- Rates are set by the Solo Bank Asset-Liability Committee (ALCO) and reviewed monthly.
- Rate changes for variable-rate accounts take effect immediately upon announcement; no advance notice is required.
- For accounts earning tiered rates, each balance tier earns the corresponding APY (not blended).
