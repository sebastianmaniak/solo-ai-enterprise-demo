# Mortgage Rates

**Effective Date:** April 1, 2026
**Next Review:** May 1, 2026

## Overview

The following mortgage rates are offered by Solo Bank for residential property purchases and refinances. Rates are determined by the borrower's credit tier (see [Credit Score Tiers](../policies/credit-score-tiers.md)), loan product, and relationship duration. All rates are subject to change without notice and are locked at the time of application.

## 30-Year Fixed Rate Mortgage

| Credit Tier | Score Range | Rate | APR* |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 — Excellent | 800–850 | 6.125% | 6.284% |
| Tier 2 — Very Good | 740–799 | 6.375% | 6.534% |
| Tier 3 — Good | 670–739 | 6.875% | 7.041% |
| Tier 4 — Fair | 580–669 | 7.500% | 7.689% |

*APR includes origination fees and assumes a $300,000 loan amount with 20% down payment.

**Tier 5 (300–579):** Not eligible for mortgage products.

## 5/1 Adjustable Rate Mortgage (ARM)

| Credit Tier | Score Range | Initial Rate | APR* |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 — Excellent | 800–850 | 5.750% | 7.102% |
| Tier 2 — Very Good | 740–799 | 6.000% | 7.352% |
| Tier 3 — Good | 670–739 | 6.500% | 7.862% |

*APR reflects the estimated cost over the life of the loan, including rate adjustments after the initial fixed period.

**Tier 4 and Tier 5:** Not eligible for ARM products.

### ARM Adjustment Details

- **Index:** 1-Year U.S. Treasury (CMT)
- **Margin:** 2.75%
- **Initial Adjustment Cap:** 2.00% above initial rate
- **Subsequent Adjustment Cap:** 2.00% per adjustment period
- **Lifetime Cap:** 5.00% above initial rate
- **Adjustment Frequency:** Annually after initial 5-year fixed period

## Relationship Discounts

Existing Solo Bank customers in good standing may qualify for a rate discount based on their account tenure:

| Relationship Duration | Rate Discount |
|---|---|
| 5–9 years | -0.125% |
| 10+ years | -0.250% |

Relationship discounts are applied to the rates shown above. For example, a Tier 2 customer with a 10-year relationship would receive a 30-year fixed rate of 6.375% - 0.250% = **6.125%**.

## Discount Points

Borrowers may purchase discount points to reduce their interest rate:

- **1 point** = 1% of the loan amount = approximately 0.250% rate reduction
- **Maximum points:** 3 points
- Points must be paid at closing
- Points are generally tax-deductible (consult a tax advisor)

### Points Example

A Tier 2 borrower taking a $400,000 30-year fixed mortgage at 6.375%:
- 1 point ($4,000) reduces rate to approximately 6.125%
- 2 points ($8,000) reduces rate to approximately 5.875%

## Rate Lock Options

| Lock Period | Fee |
|---|---|
| 30 days | No additional cost |
| 45 days | No additional cost |
| 60 days | 0.125% of loan amount |
| 75 days | 0.250% of loan amount |

Rate lock extensions: 0.125% per 15-day extension beyond the original lock period.

## Important Disclosures

- Rates shown assume a primary residence purchase with a single-family home.
- Investment properties and multi-unit properties may have different rates (add 0.500%–0.750%).
- Private Mortgage Insurance (PMI) is required for down payments less than 20% and is not reflected in the rates above.
- All loans subject to credit approval and underwriting per [Mortgage Lending Policy](../policies/mortgage-lending.md).
- Rates are not guaranteed until locked. Market conditions may cause rates to change at any time.
